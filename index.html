Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3–7 business days. Express delivery is available at checkout and delivers in 1–2 business days depending on your location.
Are all products authentic?
Absolutely. Cart Craze partners only with verified and trusted suppliers. Every product listed on our platform undergoes a strict quality and authenticity check before going live.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, you can return it within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.
How do I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive an email and SMS with a tracking link. You can also track your order directly from your Cart Craze account dashboard.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping fees and delivery times vary by location and are calculated at checkout.
